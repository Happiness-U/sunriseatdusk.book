ABOUT THE AUTHOR

ADAMSON JEREMIAH ADAMSON
AUTHOR OF "SUNRISE AT DUSK"

I love writing as a form of expression. I've found light and knowledge from books others
have written and have navigated life so far by the truths I have learned from
the pages of wonderful literary works. I dare not place myself where great writers
have passed, but I hope that somehow my work has left you with some meaning,
realization or pleasure. I thank you for taking this journey and seeing the world
through my eyes for just a moment
